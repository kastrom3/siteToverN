Absolver 1.06 уже доступен + новые маски

20 СЕНТЯБРЯ 2017

Мы только что загрузили обновление 1.06 на всех платформах, и оно будет выпущено сегодня! Извините за задержку с этим, пара ошибок заняла немного больше времени, чем ожидалось. Полные примечания к патчу приведены ниже. Основные исправления — это проблема с потерей способностей/предметов, сбои во время заставок с боссами и разрыв соединения во время боевых испытаний, которые теперь должны происходить гораздо реже.

Если у вас возникли новые проблемы после этого обновления или предыдущие проблемы все еще возникают, сообщите нам об этом по адресу support_PC@sloclap.com / support_PS4@sloclap.com , указав, из какого региона вы играете!

We know that it can be frustrating to get duplicates as rewards in Combat Trials, and we wanted to thank everyone for their support until now, so we’ve added new masks in the Guidance HUB; rest assured we’re also working hard at reworking the loot system and the end-game rewards, but in the meantime, we wanted to make new masks available to everyone. These masks are inspired by Devolver games, and we think they’re pretty cool, so we hope you like them too ! These masks won’t be there forever, so grab them while you can :)

Thanks again,
Sloclap Team

Code

  • Fixed power loss issue (powers will be given back to the player)

Optimization

  • Fixed bugs in schools menu
  • Fixed item loss issue in inventory menu
  • Fixed crash in cutscene
  • Improved Combat Trials disconnection issue
  • General Stability improvement

Design

Special abilities

absorb successful :

  • increase ghost damage refill (x2)
  • decrease stamina bonus (50 -> 20)
  • add +20 protection during 5s

Avoid successful :

  • decrease stamina bonus (40 -> 35) increase stamina cost 5 -> 10

Character avoided :

  • Decrease stamina refill (x0.3) during 1.5s
  • Stamina malus (-10)

Parry successful :

  • decrease stamina bonus (30 -> 15)

Stagger style :

  • decrease range of side special attacks (190 -> 145)

Attacks

  • Decrease range and impact on guard of fastest attacks
  • Increase impact on guard of Breaking attacks

1v1

  • health refill on kill now take in account the health ratio of both players at the beginning of the round

Others

  • Increase weight/mobility impact on stamina
  • Increase dodge iFrames (2 -> 4)
  • Keep shards after death

Shockwave

  • cost 2 shards
  • decrease range
  • add stamina bonus + boost stamina refill during 5s

Exploration

  • Decreased maximum number of enemy AIs spawned at the same time
  • Improved some enemy AIs detection range
  • Fixed navigation issue in Oratian Quarter zone

1v1

  • Fixed out of bounds issue in Adalian Columbary arena
  • Modification on the cross arena

UI

  • “Victory” & “Defeat” localised
  • Corrected misspelled english texts

  • School creation: added notification confirmation when school is successfully created
  • Social menu: “offline”/“online” information replaced by a colored dot

Art

  • Collision and navigation bugfixes
  • Performance Optimization

Audio
